Pan-Asian Screen Collective

The Pan-Asian Screen Collective (P-ASC) launched in August 2018 to represent, provide advocacy and facilitate opportunities for Pan-Asian screen practitioners on and behind New Zealand’s screens. As of 2022, PASC’s 800+ -strong membership continues to climb.
