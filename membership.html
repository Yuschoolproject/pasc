MEMBERSHIP

The Pan-Asian Screen Collective (P-ASC) supports emerging and experienced screen practitioners of pan-Asian heritage in Aotearoa/New Zealand.

Our goal is for New Zealand’s cultural landscape to honestly and equitably reflect pan-Asian faces, creativity, expertise, experience and history on screen and behind the camera.

We advocate for fairness, representation and equity in funding, and support the development of career pathways in the screen industries.

Our job is to work with New Zealand's screen sector to understand and value the diversity of experience and stories pan-Asian New Zealanders offer.

We will have succeeded when we see and hear more of our many communities’ stories and storytellers in the broader and evolving narratives of Aotearoa/ New Zealand.

Your membership is essential for our success in achieving this goal.
